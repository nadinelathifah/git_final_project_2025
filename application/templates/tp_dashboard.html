Tips on how you can get more bookings!

counter_1Set up your profile

Customers are way more likely to book someone with a complete profile. Showcase your skills, certifications & potential — it helps build trust right away.

counter_2Update your status with an enthusiastic message!

Let customers know you're active and ready to go! A dynamic message shows you're engaged, approachable, and open for business. A little personality goes a long way!

counter_3Collect reviews from happy customers :)

A couple of five-star reviews can go a long way. After a job well done, kindly ask your client to leave a quick review. It’s one of the best ways to build credibility fast.

counter_4Respond Quickly to Messages & Booking Requests

Speed matters! The faster you reply, the more likely you are to secure the booking. Even a short “Hi! Thanks for getting in touch, I’ll follow up shortly” shows professionalism.

counter_5Be Friendly & Professional On the Job

Word of mouth is powerful. Great customer experiences lead to repeat work and referrals — and you never know who they’ll recommend you to next!

counter_6Keep Learning & Improving

Stay sharp! Whether it’s picking up a new skill, investing in better tools, or improving customer service, small improvements can lead to big growth.
