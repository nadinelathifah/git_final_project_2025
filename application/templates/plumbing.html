Plumbing services {{ icon }}

Here at My Home Heroes we offer a wide range of plumbing services to keep your home flowing smoothly. Whether you’re dealing with a sneaky leak or your shower's suddenly decided it’s a sprinkler, we’ve got your back.

Explore the services below to see what we can help you with!

Services


Leaky Taps & Fixtures

That constant dripping sound isn't just annoying—it adds up on your water bill and can cause hidden damage. We'll fix or replace the faulty bits, and you’ll be back to silent sinks in no time.

Blocked Drains

Whether it’s a slow kitchen sink or a total bathroom backup, blocked drains can get messy fast. We use professional tools (and not just a bent wire hanger) to clear the blockage safely and efficiently.

Toilet Repairs

Running toilets, blockages, or mystery flushes? We’ll investigate, diagnose, and repair all toilet troubles—without judgment (we’ve seen it all, promise).

Emergency Call-Outs

Plumbing problems rarely happen at convenient times. That’s why we offer emergency call-outs—so whether it’s 10 AM or 10 PM, you’re not left with a flood and a frantic Google search.

Appliance Hook-Ups

Just got a new dishwasher or washing machine? We’ll install it safely, check the connections, and make sure it’s running properly before we leave.

Burst Pipes

A burst pipe is a serious issue. We offer rapid response to stop leaks, replace damaged sections, and help dry things out to prevent long-term damage.

Boiler & Heater Fixes

No hot water on a cold morning is a nightmare. We handle boiler servicing, repairs, and replacements so your hot showers stay sacred.

Shower Installations

Whether you're upgrading your bathroom or just replacing a worn-out unit, we install all kinds of showers with an eye for neatness, waterproofing, and water pressure.
